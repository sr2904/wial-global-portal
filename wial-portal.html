The Action Learning Solution

Action Learning involves a small group working on real problems, taking action, and learning as individuals and as a team. It builds leadership capability while solving real organisational challenges.

Certifications

WIAL is the world's leading certifying body for Action Learning

WIAL certifies coaches at four levels. All coaches must renew certification every 2 years by completing continuing education hours and submitting a recertification application.

CALC
Certified Action Learning Coach
Entry-level · 32+ hours training · 2-year renewal cycle · 2 supervised practice sessions
PALC
Professional AL Coach
100+ coaching hours · Advanced facilitation · Cross-sector experience
SALC
Senior AL Coach
Can train & certify new coaches · Cohort management · Multi-country delivery
MALC
Master AL Coach
Highest level · Global thought leadership · Organisation-wide programmes

📋 Recertification

All certifications require renewal every 2 years. Coaches must complete 32+ continuing education hours, submit practice session records, and complete a recertification application form via the portal. Reminders are sent 3 months, 2 months, and 1 month before the deadline.
